The Local Market Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy is for you. The Local Market and Local Market are committed to protecting your privacy. Because we hate junk email as much as anyone. This Privacy Policy tells you why we collect your personal information, what we collect and what we do with it.

 

COLLECTION OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

As a visitor to our app: If you are just browsing our app and don’t complete any online form, then we won’t be collecting personal information from you that would enable us to identify you. As a member of our app: Membership is free and only members can buy products through our website. The personal information that we ask you to provide when creating a account is your full name, email, mobile number. We ask user to provide the information such as full name, mobile number,address, landmark, and pincodeeverytime you place an order on Local Market app. We collect information that can identify you, such as your e-mail address, name, address or postal address or telephone number. Also we might collect demographic information which is not unique to you, such as your ZIP code which we may collate and use statistically. We will only collect this information directly from you.

INFORMATION FROM CHILDREN

We do not collect or maintain information from children we know are under the age of 13. No part of our website is structured to attract anyone under the age of 13. 

USE OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

Our privacy policy is very clear. We do not sell, rent or lease our customer lists to third parties.

We collect and use your personal information to operate our app and deliver the services you require from our website.

Also, we use your personal information to tell you about other products or services available from Local Market. 

We might also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

In addition, we may share data with trusted partners to help us send you email or postal mail, or provide customer support. All of those third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services for us. Also they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

We won’t provide your details to anyone else unless compelled or permitted by law. Or required by our professional advisors to complete their services to us, such as for auditing or insurance purposes. Also we will disclose your personal information, without notice, only as necessary for the following reasons…

• (a) obey the law or comply with legal process served on us.

• (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Local Market. 

• (c) act under serious and immediate circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of our website, or the public.

We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent. Also we do not collect your credit card number or other financial data about you.

 

UPDATING YOUR INFORMATION

If you are concerned that the information we hold about you needs to be updated, please update your membership details by logging in, or contact us at onlinelocalmarket@gmail.com, setting out what details you would like to have updated.

 

SECURITY OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We are careful with your personal information and secure it from unauthorized access, use or disclosure to the best of our ability.

 

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY STATEMENT

We will occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the law, company and customer feedback. Also we encourage you to periodically review these terms to be informed of how we are protecting your information.

 

CONTACT INFORMATION
